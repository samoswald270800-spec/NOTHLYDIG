Discovery

A focused working session to map the role, team dynamics, and success metrics — not just the job description.

02

Sourcing

We tap our 60,000+ pre-qualified network alongside targeted active sourcing — matching on capability, not availability.

Why Northly

The standard we hold ourselves to

Senior consultants, every time

Every mandate is owned by a consultant with 10+ years in the field. No junior hand-offs. No diluted accountability. You get the person on the phone, and that's who works your search.

3 to 5 candidates. Not 30.

We send curated shortlists — never a dump of resumes to wade through. If we're not confident in a candidate, they don't go to you. Your time is worth more than volume.

Structured, bias-aware screening

Blind evaluations, standardized interview frameworks, and inclusive language built into every step. Better process produces better hires and stronger teams.

Transparent pricing, upfront

Flat-fee or contingent — agreed before work begins. No surprise invoices, no hidden conversion fees, no post-hire complications.
