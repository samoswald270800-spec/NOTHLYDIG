How We Work

From brief to placed — in days, not months

A six-stage process refined across 4,800+ placements and a decade of practice. Built to remove ambiguity, accelerate timelines, and protect both parties.

01

Discovery & Role Brief

A focused 45-minute strategy session with your hiring lead. We map the role, team dynamics, success criteria, compensation range, and cultural context — building a true talent brief, not just a job description.

02

Market Mapping & Active Sourcing

We tap our 60,000+ pre-qualified candidate network alongside targeted active sourcing, LinkedIn outreach, and referral channels. We look for fit, not just availability.

03

Screening & Structured Evaluation

Every candidate undergoes a multi-stage evaluation: initial screening, structured behavioural interview, technical or portfolio review (where applicable), and compensation alignment.

04

Curated Shortlist Delivery

You receive 3 to 5 fully vetted, pre-interviewed candidates with written summaries, assessment notes, and our consultant's recommendation. Typically within 72 business hours.

05

Offer Management & Onboarding

We manage the offer process — compensation negotiation, reference and background checks, notice period strategy, and onboarding logistics — from your side and the candidate's side.

06

Post-Placement Support

Check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days. If a permanent placement doesn't work out within that window, we re-fill the role at no additional cost. Accountability doesn't end at the signature.

About Northly Digital

Built in Ottawa.
Grown across a nation.

Northly Digital was founded in 2014 by Sarah Okafor and Marc Beauchamp — two senior recruiters who had spent their careers inside large national firms and seen the same problem play out: clients were getting volume, not value.

They built Northly Digital with a different premise: fewer mandates, handled better. Senior consultants on every search. Honest timelines. Guaranteed outcomes.

FAQ

Common questions, straight answers

If something isn't answered here, call us directly at +1 (613) 817-4782 and we'll answer it in two minutes.

Still have questions?
How long does it take to fill a role with Northly Digital?

For most mandates, we deliver a curated shortlist of 3 to 5 pre-vetted candidates within 72 business hours of your discovery call. Total time to hire — from brief to accepted offer — averages 14 to 21 business days for permanent roles. Contract and temporary placements often move same-week.

What makes Northly Digital different from other staffing agencies?

Three things: senior consultants on every mandate (no junior sourcer hand-offs), curated shortlists of 3 to 5 candidates (not 30-resume dumps), and guaranteed outcomes. Every permanent placement comes with a 90-day replacement guarantee. We also operate on transparent, agreed-upon fees — no surprises.

Do you work with startups, or only enterprise companies?

Both. Roughly 40% of our clients are growth-stage companies (Series A through Series C) and 60% are established mid-market or enterprise organizations. The process is the same — a curated shortlist, proper vetting, and accountability after placement. The only difference is how we calibrate for culture and growth-stage expectations.

What are your fees for permanent placement?

Our permanent placement fee is based on a percentage of the hired candidate's first-year base salary, agreed upon at the start of the engagement. We offer contingent and retained arrangements depending on the role and urgency. We provide a complete fee quote during your first call — no vague ranges, no hidden costs.

Can Northly Digital support remote or US-based positions?

Yes. We place candidates in remote and hybrid roles across Canada and the US. Our New York office handles US-based mandates directly, and our Canadian offices regularly fill fully remote roles for US-headquartered companies. We also provide Employer of Record (EOR) services for cross-border situations where a local entity is needed.

What does your candidate screening process actually involve?

Every candidate we present has completed: (1) an initial screen with a Northly consultant, (2) a structured behavioural interview, (3) a skills or portfolio review where applicable, (4) compensation alignment, and (5) a preliminary reference conversation. You receive written interview notes and our consultant's recommendation alongside each résumé. Background and formal reference checks are completed on the finalist before offer.
